    About Me

    Funnily enough, setting up emulators and modding video games like Dark Souls and Skyrim were what originally led me to seriously consider computer science as a career. I figured if I had enough patience to read mod documentation for hours and suffer through troubleshooting confusing bugs, I could do the same for industry software. Turns out I was right!

    Particularly, I remember debugging a Wii64 application for Homebrew for 6 hours straight and finally getting it to work. That was the week I started avidly studying computer science.

    I'm lucky that I work in a field where I get to be a creative problem solver and feel like I'm making an impact every day. I enjoy trying out new technologies and learning new skills to add to my arsenal.
